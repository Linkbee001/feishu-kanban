# Meeting Minutes

## Goal

Capture what happened, what was decided, and what needs follow-up.

## Workflow

1. Separate facts, decisions, and open questions.
2. Extract concrete follow-up actions and owners when possible.
3. Keep the notes compact but structured.

## Output guidance

- Prefer a `document` output for reusable minutes.
- Use a `task` output if the main deliverable is action items.
